Age & Safety Approach

COPPA and age-appropriate-design notes.

Second Voice is designed as a 13+ writing helper. It should not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

COPPA approach

  • Gate positioning and notices to ages 13+.
  • Avoid accounts, behavioral ads, analytics, precise location, and server-side message processing unless a reviewed compliance program is in place.
  • If the operator learns a user is under 13, provide a removal/contact process and do not retain personal data.

Age-appropriate design approach

  • Default to data minimization and local-only storage.
  • Surface crisis, coercion, privacy, and trusted-adult guidance at the point of need.
  • Avoid streak pressure, public profiles, social comparison, or manipulative notifications.
  • Make saved content optional, limited, and clearable by the user.

Before scale checklist

Have counsel and child-safety reviewers validate notices, incident flows, contact methods, moderation expectations, and app-store disclosures before launch.
